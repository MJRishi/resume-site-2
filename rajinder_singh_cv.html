Mr. Rajinder Singh has been teaching undergraduate students for over 15 years. Currently, he is teaching as an Assistant Professor at Ram Lal Anand College, University of Delhi. He started his career from Keshav Mahavidyalya, University of Delhi in 2008 & later he joined Ram Lal Anand College, University of Delhi in 2010. He has published research papers in various reputed international & national journals & presented many research papers at international & national conferences.

Presently, he is pursuing Ph.D from the Department of Commerce, University of Delhi in the field of finance. He has been the co-authored of Business Laws Book designed for the students of Delhi University published in 2017. He has organized various academic events under different capacities.
